## <a name="set-up-bookable-resource-as-a-pricing-dimension"></a><span data-ttu-id="404fa-168">Impostare una risorsa prenotabile come dimensione di determinazione dei prezzi</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="404fa-168">Set up bookable resource as a pricing dimension</span></span>
